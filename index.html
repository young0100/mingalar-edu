Studying Abroad Shouldn't Be This Hard

Every year, thousands of Myanmar students dream of studying abroad. But the journey is full of obstacles that stop most from ever starting.

Too Much Confusion

Which country? Which university? What visa? How much does it cost? The overwhelming number of choices leads to paralysis.

  • ✗ Conflicting information from unreliable sources
  • ✗ No way to compare countries side by side

Hidden Costs & Scams

Unverified agents charge high fees with no guarantees. Families invest savings only to face visa rejections and broken promises.

  • ✗ Agencies that disappear after taking payment
  • ✗ No transparency in fees or processes

Alone After Arrival

Even those who make it abroad often struggle alone — no local support, culture shock, and nowhere to turn.

  • ✗ No on-ground support in the host country
  • ✗ Parents left worrying without updates
